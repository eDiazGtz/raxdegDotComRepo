Welcome to Ghallant

A World where enemies lurk behind every corner and shadows hold knives around every bend.

There have been rumors of a Cult of Dragons with fanatics that have been robbing towns, assaulting innocents and destroying the lives of the everyday man. The world is in chaos as many are dealt terrible hands by fate. People now cower before the darkness of the Cult. Those few who stand up against it face an onslaught of creatures and plots that threaten to take their lives and the lives of those they love.

There are various factions who work to undermine the Cult of the Dragon. Though usually these factions don't play well with each other, they have started working together in order to bring this Cult of organized crime down. Without much organization and leadership, the different factions have been working in many different directions and find themselves strained for resources.

It has only been recently, through a series of events that began in the small town of Zidurza that an unlikely group of Heroes have risen to meet the cult on various occasions. Now, with justice at their side, they have been chosen by Dragons to help bring about peace and balance to the world. No one has heard of people riding dragons since the days of old. Awestruck, the people of Starfall City watch this band of heroes grow in strength and influence as they venture to bringing about peace to the world.

If these heroes cannot bring peace, many fear that no one could...

What horrible machinations has the Cult of the Dragon for these dragon-riders who blaspheme dragons in their eyes?
